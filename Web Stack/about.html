About Us

Welcome to Iceberg Stationery, your number one source for all things stationery. We're dedicated to providing you the very best of stationery items, with an emphasis on quality, variety, and uniqueness.

Founded in 2024 by Anosh, Iceberg Stationery has come a long way from its beginnings. When Anosh first started out, their passion for "eco-friendly stationery" drove them to start their own business.

We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
